# FinOps KPI and Decision Tree

## Purpose
Help answer general FinOps questions with a consistent decision path.

## Core executive KPIs
- Total spend by scope
- Spend trend month-over-month and quarter-over-quarter
- Forecast variance
- Budget variance
- Allocation coverage
- Tag compliance on spend
- Top anomalies and mean time to assign and resolve
- Commitment coverage and utilization, if applicable
- Cost per business unit metric

## Decision tree
### Question type: visibility
Use when the issue is “what happened?”
- check spend by account, team, product, environment, service
- validate allocation and tag quality
- identify top deltas and top drivers

### Question type: forecasting
Use when the issue is “what is likely to happen?”
- compare actuals vs prior forecast
- update assumptions for growth, seasonality, launches, and infrastructure changes
- estimate end-of-month and end-of-quarter outcomes

### Question type: value
Use when the issue is “is this worth it?”
- tie spend to product, customer, transaction, or workload metrics
- compute cost per unit and trend
- compare with target or strategic goal

### Question type: optimization
Use when the issue is “what should we change?”
- check idle usage, oversized resources, storage lifecycle, commitment gaps, scheduling opportunities
- rank actions by impact, effort, and risk

### Question type: governance
Use when the issue is “how do we prevent repeat issues?”
- check policy compliance
- identify missing owners or approvals
- define stronger thresholds, workflows, or automation
