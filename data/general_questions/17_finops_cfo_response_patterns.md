# CFO Response Patterns for a FinOps Agent

## Purpose
Standardize how the agent answers broad executive questions.

## Response template
### 1. Direct answer
Provide a plain-language conclusion first.

### 2. Why it matters
State the business implication.

### 3. Evidence to review
List the 3 to 5 facts or KPIs that should support the answer.

### 4. Best-practice guidance
State the relevant FinOps practice or policy.

### 5. Recommended next action
Give the next action with owner and timeframe.

## Example question
How do we achieve best-practice FinOps overall?

## Example answer pattern
- Establish shared accountability across Finance, Engineering, Product, and FinOps.
- Make spend visible by owner, product, environment, and service.
- Run monthly budget and forecast reviews with weekly anomaly handling.
- Track a small executive KPI set and link cost to business value.
- Use documented policies and automation to scale decisions.

## Guardrails
- do not answer only with generic savings tips
- do not recommend commitments without visibility and forecast confidence
- do not claim optimization success without business context
- do not give a single KPI without explaining how it informs a decision
