# FinOps Monthly Business Review Template

## Purpose
Provide a standard monthly review structure for leadership, finance, engineering, and product.

## Audience
- CFO
- Finance business partner
- FinOps lead
- Engineering leadership
- Platform owners
- Product or business owners where relevant

## Standard agenda
1. Last month actuals
2. Current month run rate
3. Forecast to month end and quarter end
4. Top cost drivers
5. Budget variance
6. Commitment posture
7. Optimization actions and owner status
8. Risks, exceptions, and decisions required

## Minimum scorecard
- total amortized spend
- month-over-month percentage change
- budget variance
- top three services by spend
- top three scopes by variance
- allocation coverage percentage
- commitment coverage and utilization
- unit economics metric for major platforms

## Discussion rules
- separate noise from meaningful variance
- distinguish growth-driven spend from waste
- identify one-time events explicitly
- assign owners for corrective actions
- close prior action items before creating new ones

## Output expected from the assistant
For review support, the assistant should produce:
- concise executive summary
- bullet list of drivers
- decision-focused recommendations
- unresolved questions requiring human validation
