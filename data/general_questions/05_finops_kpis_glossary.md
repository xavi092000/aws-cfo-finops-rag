# FinOps KPI and Glossary Reference

## Purpose

This document standardizes common FinOps metrics and terms for consistent retrieval.

## KPI references

### Monthly cloud spend

Total technology spend for a defined period and scope.

### Variance to budget

Difference between actual spend and planned budget.

### Forecast accuracy

Difference between forecasted and actual spend.

### Cost per workload

Allocated spend divided by the number of tracked workloads.

### Cost per dashboard

Allocated spend divided by the number of active dashboards or reporting assets.

### Cost per pipeline run

Allocated spend divided by pipeline execution count.

### Commitment coverage

Share of eligible spend covered by Savings Plans or Reserved Instances.

### Commitment utilization

Degree to which purchased commitments are actually consumed.

### Tagging coverage

Share of spend attached to complete and valid business tags.

### Untagged spend

Spend that cannot be assigned accurately because of missing or invalid tags.

## Glossary

### Amortized cost

Cost distributed across the time period of a commitment so that committed spend is represented more evenly.

### Unblended cost

Direct line-item cost without blended averaging.

### Idle resource

A resource that incurs cost while delivering low or no useful value.

### Rightsizing

Adjusting resource size to better match actual demand.

### Allocation

Assigning shared or direct spend to business-relevant scopes.

### Unit economics

Cost measured per business or operational unit, such as per customer, product, dataset, or pipeline.

### Anomaly

A cost or usage pattern that deviates meaningfully from expected behavior.

\## Unit Economics (FinOps)



Unit economics in FinOps refers to measuring cloud cost per unit of business value.



A unit can be:

\- a user

\- a transaction

\- a product feature

\- a data pipeline run



This allows organizations to understand how cloud spending scales with business activity and supports cost efficiency decisions.





\## Common FinOps KPIs



Common FinOps KPIs include:

\- cost per unit

\- total cloud spend

\- budget variance

\- forecast accuracy

\- cost allocation coverage



These KPIs help organizations track financial performance and optimization progress.
